Frequently Asked Questions
How do I connect my Gmail account?
Navigate to Settings > Integrations, and click "Connect" next to Google Gmail. You will be prompted to authorize access. Once connected, ShipFlow automatically processes shipping confirmation emails.
What is the tracking limit on the Starter plan?
The Starter plan includes 100 monthly tracking credits. You can check your current monthly usage directly in the sidebar account footer or by going to Settings > Usage.
How do exception statuses get updated?
Exceptions are parsed automatically by AI when a carrier reports a delivery delay or failure. You can assign owners to exceptions, set a priority, and mark them resolved once resolved.
